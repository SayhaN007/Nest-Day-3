About

India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area, the most populous democracy in the world, and the most populous democracy in Asia. Located in the Indian Ocean, it shares land borders with Pakistan to the west, China to the northeast, Nepal to the southeast, and Bangladesh to the east. In the Indian Ocean, India is in the vicinity of Sri Lanka and the Maldives; its Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime border with Thailand and Indonesia.
